Yi Yang 杨毅

I am Yi Yang, currently a Ph.D. researcher in Electrical and Computer Engineering at University of Macau, advised by Prof. Feng Wan.

My research focuses on Brain-Computer Interfaces (BCIs), EEG/fNIRS signal analysis, affective computing, and deep learning. I am particularly interested in building adaptive, robust, and privacy-preserving neural interface systems.

Recently, I have been working on class-incremental learning, few-shot learning, domain adaptation, and analytic recursive learning for EEG-based BCIs, including emotion recognition, motor imagery, SSVEP, sleep monitoring, EEG-to-image, and EEG-to-text.
